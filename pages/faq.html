Frequently Asked Questions
What is Make in BVB?
Make in BVB is an entrepreneurial cell of BVB (KLE TECHNOLOGICAL UNIVERSITY). Team make in BVB is an independent student body which works under CTIE(Centre for Technology Innovation and Entrepreneurship) where all the tasks from maintaining team dynamics to organizing events is carried out by the students independently.
What does Make in BVB do?
Make in BVB organises events related to entrepreneurship and product development and MiB also mentors participants in such events. It acts like a bridge between students and entrepreneurship.
