About HEX
Hubli ESDM Exchange(HEX)launch was the first state level event by Team Make in BVB of the academic year 2020-21 in collaboration with CTIE(Centre for Technology Innovation and Entrepreneurship), Government of Karnataka and India electronics and semiconductors association(ISEA) . HEX is an incubation centre mainly for Electronic System Design and Manufacturing (ESDM) startups with live monitoring and facilities for startups. The motto of the launch of HEX was to give insights about entrepreneurship and product development.

There were about 100 people from different domains who were part of this launch. There were students, startup founders,principals,companies from different parts of the state .It was presented by eminent speakers including MD of Karnataka Information Technology Services, Chairman of India Semiconductors and Electronics Limited,Vice chancellor of KLE Technological University,Director of CTIE and HEX and founders of top companies.
