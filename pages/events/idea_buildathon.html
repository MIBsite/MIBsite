About KLE Idea buildathon
Idea-buildathon is an event by Make in BVB(MiB) in collaboration with KLETECH's Developer Students Club(DSC) supported by Google. It is an innovative hackathon for enthusiastic problem solvers. This event executes in three phases which are : Think->Pitch->Build .

This event is an intriguing amalgam of Hardware and software realisation spread across in different domains.

Phase 1 - Idea Proposal
Phase 2 - Idea pitching
Phase 3 - Product realisation

The products which turn out well will be offered incubation in KLECTIE, Hubballi.
