About PUPA
Pupa is a mega event of Make in BVB(MiB) where students from all over the state get exposure to learn multiple skills ranging from product development to marketing to communication skills. MiB provides mentorship and also seed funding for product development and PUPA gives them the platform to showcase their products.

The students are mentored extensively for product development and also motivated when they feel low in the process . Along with this, students are also guided regarding the marketing of their product. PUPA gives students a great opportunity to interact with investors and if the product is unique and innovative then investors give them fundings too .

PUPA helps students to widen their horizon of knowledge in every sector. This gives them an opportunity to stand tall amongst the crowd. In PUPA 2019 there were approximately 500 students from all over Karnataka and other states who participated and enjoyed the event.
Frequently Asked Questions
Should we sell our products on exhibition day?
The exhibition is only meant to showcase your product to your fellow participants and audience though some sales could be made on that day but at least one of the products have to be sold beforehand.
Should we return the Seed Capital Given?
If you showcase your product on the day of the exhibition and have sold at least one of the products beforehand, there is no need to return the seed capital, else you would be asked to return it.
What exactly happens on the day of the exhibition?
All the participants showcase the products that they have created. VC, teachers, news reporters and possible investors look around for the best product.
What should we do AFTER PUPA?
Pupa is just the beginning. It’s a platform to give you a head start on your way to become an Entrepreneur.
Will the college provide funds for our startup that arises from PUPA?
If your product was really good and you can prove the same Team MIB then you have a good amount of chance to be incubated and have an on-campus Start-up.
What is the Deadline?
The product should be ready and functioning a week before PUPA and have to sold before the exhibition day i.e. 19th October 2019.
Can the products be Artistic?
Yes and no. If the product is artistic but also has some technical factors then yes, it is accepted. If it is something that is purely artistic like jewelry or painting then it is not allowed.
Can we sell food items?
NO.
What if no one buys our product?
It's okay, we want you to try your best and gain experience.
Can we sell our products outside the college?
Definitely yes, Make a bill too!
Should the product be made under Rs 500?
No, you and your teammates can chip in.
Do we have to refund Rs 500 amount after Pupa?
Yes, if you do not show up for exhibition.
How do we have to decide about the quantity of the products that have to be make ?
It’s up to you, use those awesome brains of yours, apply some profit, loss formulas and take your own decisions.
